# Loan-Approval-Credit-Risk-Analysis-Dashboard
This Power BI interactive dashboard analyses loan application data to identify the primary drivers of loan approval decisions and assess potential credit risk segments.
The objective is to simulate a credit risk analysis workflow by exploring borrower characteristics, approval patterns, and exposure concentration.

# Business Objective

To determine:

What drives loan approvals?

Which borrower segments represent higher risk?

How approval rates vary across demographic and financial attributes.

# Key Findings

Credit history is the dominant factor influencing loan approval.

Graduates have higher approval rates than non-graduates.

Semiurban applicants show the strongest approval performance.

Self-employment and gender show limited impact on approval probability.

Loan exposure is concentrated in mid-sized loan amounts.

# Business Recommendation

Maintain credit history as the primary underwriting filter.

Apply geographic segmentation in portfolio strategy.

Monitor demographic approval trends for fairness and risk balance.

Explore predictive modeling for enhanced credit scoring.
